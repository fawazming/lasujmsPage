About the Journal

A Multidisciplinary Hub for Management Excellence

LASU Journal of Management Sciences (LASUJMS) is a multidisciplinary peer-reviewed international Journal of the Faculty of Management Sciences, Lagos State University, Lagos.

Published twice a year in March and December, LASUJMS accepts qualitative and quantitative papers, reports from the field, critical review papers, and discussions concerning the main features of management practices and behaviour.

  • Double-blind peer review process
  • International editorial board
  • Bi-annual publication schedule
  • Focus on practical & theoretical insights
